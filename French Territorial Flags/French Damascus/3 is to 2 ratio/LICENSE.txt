EDITION:
--------
1. Firstly, I made French canton by the State of Aleppo Flag, 
   made transparent everything outside of canton. (1st Layer).
2. Secondly, I removed State of Damascus flag's French ensign. 
   Then I copied that part.
3. Thirdly, I used 2nd layer alongside with 1st layer and pasted 
   that on that 2nd layer.
4. Finally, I have made 1st layer as front and 2nd layer as back.
